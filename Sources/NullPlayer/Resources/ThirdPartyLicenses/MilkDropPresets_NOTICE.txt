Bundled MilkDrop / projectM Presets

NullPlayer bundles a curated set of MilkDrop-format (.milk) visualization
presets that are rendered by the projectM engine. These presets are community
works originally authored for Winamp's MilkDrop and for projectM, and are
distributed as part of the widely circulated MilkDrop / projectM preset
collections (e.g. the "Cream of the Crop" packs).

Attribution: each preset embeds its author(s) in the file name (for example
"amandio c - ...", "Geiss - ...", "Adam - ..."). The original preset authors
retain credit for their work. Presets are shared freely by the MilkDrop /
projectM community for use with compatible visualizers.

The projectM preset collections are distributed by the projectM project:
https://github.com/projectM-visualizer/presets-cream-of-the-crop

No preset author endorses NullPlayer. Presets are included for interoperability
with the projectM rendering engine bundled with this application.
