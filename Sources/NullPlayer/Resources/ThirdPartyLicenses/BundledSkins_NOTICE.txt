Bundled Skins and Visual Assets

The modern skins bundled with NullPlayer (ArcticMinimal, BananaParty,
BloodGlass, Bubblegum Retro, EmeraldForge, ForgedTitanium, HyperPopPrism,
IndustrialSignal, NeonWave, Sakura Minimal, SeaGlass, Skulls, SmoothGlass)
are original assets created for NullPlayer and are distributed under the
NullPlayer project license.

The bundled classic skin "NullPlayer-Silver.wsz" uses the Winamp 2.x classic
skin format. The classic skin format and sprite layout originate from the
Winamp skinning specification; the bundled skin's artwork is an original work
produced for NullPlayer.

No third-party copyrighted skin artwork is redistributed without permission.
If you believe a bundled asset infringes your rights, please open an issue on
the project repository so it can be removed or re-attributed.
